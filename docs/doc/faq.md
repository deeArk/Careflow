# ❓ CareFlow FAQ (Outpatient System)

## 📌 Overview

CareFlow is an **outpatient-only healthcare management system** designed to manage patient flow from registration through consultation, services, billing, payment, and completion.

> ⚠️ Important: CareFlow does NOT support inpatient admission, ward management, or hospitalisation workflows.

---

## 🧾 Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Is CareFlow for admitted (inpatient) patients?
No. CareFlow is strictly for **outpatients only**. It does not support admission, inpatient care, or ward/bed management.

---

### 2. What types of patients can be managed in CareFlow?
CareFlow supports only outpatient cases such as:
- Walk-in patients
- Appointment-based visits
- Follow-up consultations
- Minor outpatient procedures

---

### 3. Does CareFlow support emergency admissions?
No. Emergency and inpatient cases should be handled outside CareFlow in a full hospital EMR system.

---

### 4. Can CareFlow work offline?
Yes.

- **Offline Mode:** Patient registration, queue management, and basic data entry  
- **Online Mode:** Required for syncing data, billing, payments, and full system functionality

---

### 5. What happens if the internet disconnects during use?
The system continues in offline mode. Once internet is restored, data automatically syncs.

---

### 6. What is the patient workflow in CareFlow?
The standard workflow is:

**Registration → Queue → Appointment → Consultation → Services → Invoice → Payment → Completion**

---

### 7. Can a patient skip steps in the workflow?
No. The system enforces structured workflow stages. However, authorized staff may override in exceptional cases.

---

### 8. Who uses CareFlow?
CareFlow supports multiple roles:
- Reception staff  
- Nurses  
- Doctors  
- Cashiers (Billing Officers)  
- Administrators  

---

### 9. What if a patient is not found in the system?
You can search using:
- Patient ID  
- Phone number  

Or register the patient as a new outpatient.

---

### 10. Does CareFlow support prescriptions?
Yes. Prescriptions can be created during consultation and stored in patient records for future reference.

---

## 📞 Support

For technical issues:
- Contact your system administrator
